## 🌊 About Oceanside, CA

Oceanside is a vibrant coastal city in **San Diego County, California**, known for:

- 🏖️ **Oceanside Pier** — The longest wooden pier on the West Coast (1,954 feet)
- 🏛️ **Mission San Luis Rey de Francia** — California's largest historic mission
- 🏄 **California Surf Museum** — First surf museum in the United States
- 🎬 **Top Gun House** — Historic Queen Anne cottage from the 1986 movie
- ⚓ **Oceanside Harbor Village** — Waterfront dining, shops, and water sports
- 🎭 **Downtown Oceanside** — Cultural hub with galleries, restaurants, and events
- 🏕️ **Camp Pendleton** — Major Marine Corps base on northern border
